Pair Programming

Pair programming is a software development technique in which two programmers work together at one workstation.

The goal of pair programming is to produce higher quality code, by having two people work together to review each other's work and to catch any errors or bugs.

Pair programming can also be used as a teaching tool, as it allows more experienced programmers to share their knowledge with less experienced programmers.
